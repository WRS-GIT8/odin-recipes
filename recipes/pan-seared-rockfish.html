Pan Seared Rockfish

Crisp rockfish basted in herb butter

Easy dinner for tender yet crispy fish that is filled with flavor

Ingredients

  • Fish fillets - 2 6oz pieces
  • Oil for frying - 3tbsp
  • Butter - 2tbsp
  • Thyme sprigs - 4
  • Lemon wedges - 2
  • Garlic - half clove
  • Salt - to taste

Steps

  1. Pat both sides of each fillet dry with a paper towel
  2. Season each side with salt
  3. Preheat the cast iron skillet to a high heat
  4. Add oil to the heated pan
  5. Place the fillets skin side down
  6. Reduce to medium heat and cook for 4 mins
  7. Flip and add butter, garlic and thyme. Stir
  8. Tilt the pan then baste the fish with a spoon
  9. Baste occassionally for another 2 minutes
  10. Squeeze the lemon wedge over the fillet and serve
