No Knead Bread

Simple wonderful bread

This beginner friendly recipe will have you baking fantastic bread with a cripsy crust and fluffy interior with ease.

Ingredients

  • Flour - 3 cups
  • Salt - 2 teaspoons
  • Active dry yeast - ¾ teaspoon
  • Luke warm water - 1 ½ cups

Steps

  1. Combine yeast and warm water then stir
  2. In a large bowl mix the flour, salt and yeast mixture together until it forms a dough
  3. Cover the dough and let rise for 6 - 8 hours
  4. Shape the dough into a tight ball. Recover and let sit for 1 hour
  5. Preheat the Dutch oven to 450F for 30 mins
  6. Carefully add the dough to the Dutch oven
  7. Bake covered for 30mins
  8. Remove lid and bake for 15 mins
  9. Let the bread sit outside of the Dutch oven for 15mins before slicing
